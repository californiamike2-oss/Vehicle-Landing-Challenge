CUSTOMER PROFILE:
"Meet Marcus, a 42-year-old landscape architect and father of three who needs a vehicle that can bridge the gap between "family shuttle" and "professional workhorse." His daily routine involves navigating suburban school drop-offs followed by visits to active construction sites where he frequently carries heavy stone samples, architectural plans, and sometimes a small utility trailer. Marcus is currently driving an aging mid-size SUV that feels cramped during weekend camping trips and struggles with the weight of his equipment. He needs a vehicle with a massive interior volume, a high towing capacity (at least 7,000 lbs), and a rugged enough drivetrain to handle unpaved job sites without sacrificing the "curb appeal" necessary when meeting high-end clients."
